Exhale and hold for 30 secs..

Inhale and hold for 15 seconds

The Wim Hof technique, is a controlled hyperventiallation that stimulates the sympathetic nervous system.

Lying flat or seated, take 30 very deep breaths, through the nose, into the pit of the stomach. The movement of each inhalation should feel like a wave fuilling the stomach and up through the lungs

After 30 breaths leave approximately 1/4 of the air in your lungs and hold your breath for one minute

After a minute or once you've reached your breath hold limit, take one large breath and hold for 15 seconds

The process can be completed 3 - 5 times
